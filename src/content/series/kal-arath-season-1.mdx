Garen Vosque finished his time as a soldier with one thing on his mind, captaining his own battle barge. He befriends an unlikly ally and stumbles into the middle of a war between two rival doomsday cults.

This is a solo actual play series using the Kal Arath TTRPG rules by Castle Grief. Sessions played live, recorded as they happen. Nothing scripted, and everything is emergent play from dice, oracles, and tables.
